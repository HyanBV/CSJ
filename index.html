We Make Science Matter!

Who We Are...

We are a devoted group of researchers, investigators and scientifically-talented people with the main goal of sharing young talent in the scientific and technological areas. We were established in 2003, and have been publishing amazing young talent ever since! The best articles are published each March every year!

CSJ 2021 Fest

The CSJ Fest takes place every March every year, where more than 700 articles are submitted, but only a few are published. All published articles are then offered the opportunity to work on the subject with even more-experienced scientists and tech experts!
